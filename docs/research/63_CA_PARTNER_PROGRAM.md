# 63 — CA Partner Program (Phase 6 One-Pager)

> Year-1 is **not** a Computax clone. Partners get clean client prep + evidence packs; firms keep professional judgment and portal filing.

**Status:** ✅ EXECUTED (Jul 2026)  
**Implements:** Phase 6 exit item — CA partner one-pager  
**Audience:** Practitioners evaluating a co-pilot seat or lead-share pilot

---

## One-liner

**LastMinute ITR prepares salaried (and simple ITR-2/3/4) cases with documents, reconcile, and an evidence pack — you review and file.**

---

## Who it's for

| Fit | Not a fit (yet) |
| --- | --- |
| Solo / small firm drowning in Form16 intake | Full-service Computax replacement |
| Firms that want clients to self-prep before review | Firms needing HUF / trust / international tax modules |
| CAs who want fewer "WhatsApp photo of Form16" threads | Anyone expecting us to e-file on their behalf |

---

## Tiered program

| Tier | What you get | What we need | Economics (V1 proposal) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Lead share** | Qualified DIY users who hit CA escalation (out of scope, low confidence, user request) | Valid membership proof; response SLA ≤2 business days | Revenue share on converted consult (negotiated; transparent) |
| **Co-pilot seats** | Multi-client dashboard, evidence packs, bulk export hooks | Firm email domain; 1 named partner | Per-seat monthly during season; off-season pause option |
| **Firm plans** | Branded client links, priority support, onboarding call | ≥3 seats; compliance contact | Annual firm plan; volume discount |

**No spam leads.** Escalations only when the product constitution says a human CA is required (doc 50 §6).

---

## What the evidence pack contains

1. Profile + recommended ITR form + routing reasons  
2. Income heads and regime comparison (old vs new) with engine trace rule ids  
3. Risk flags and open mismatches (AIS / Form16 / 26AS)  
4. Recommendations with proof-required lists (lawful only)  
5. Brought-forward losses / depreciation schedule when applicable  
6. Confidence score and missing documents  

Format: JSON (API) + printable summary (PDF in V1.1).

## Practitioner workflow (11-step Computax map — where we help)

| Step | Computax-style work | LastMinute ITR role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Intake | Collect Form16 / AIS | Client uploads; we parse |
| Reconcile | Match TDS and income | Mismatch-first UI |
| Heads | Salary, HP, CG, business | Engine + Smart CA questions |
| Deductions | Chapter VI-A discovery | SmartSavingsFinder |
| Compute | Tax + regime | Deterministic engine (AY ruleset) |
| Review | CA judgment | **You** — evidence pack |
| File | Portal / utility | Client or you on ITD portal |
| E-verify | Client OTP | Companion checklist |

We **displace grunt intake**, not professional sign-off.

## Requirements to join

1. Active membership with ICAI (or equivalent practice proof)  
2. Agree to no-spam and data-handling terms (DPDP-aligned)  
3. Named compliance contact for escalations  
4. No resale of leads to third parties
