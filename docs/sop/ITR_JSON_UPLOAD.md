# SOP: Upload ITR JSON On The Income Tax Portal

## Goal

Use LastMinute ITR to prepare a portal-ready JSON file, then upload it yourself on `incometax.gov.in`.

## Important honesty note

LastMinute ITR does not e-file or submit your return. We prepare the JSON and guide you. You upload, preview, submit, and e-verify on the government portal.

## Before you start

- Paid plan unlocked.
- No blocking mismatches in the app.
- JSON export generated for the correct ITR form and assessment year.
- PAN, Aadhaar OTP access, and portal login ready.

## Steps

1. In LastMinute ITR, go to `/file/companion`.
2. Click `Download JSON` when the app says validations are clear.
3. Log in to the Income Tax portal.
4. Choose `e-File` → `Income Tax Returns` → `File Income Tax Return`.
5. Select `AY 2026-27`.
6. Choose upload/pre-filled JSON mode if the portal offers it for your form.
7. Upload the JSON downloaded from LastMinute ITR.
8. Wait for portal validation.
9. If the portal reports errors, do not guess. Return to LastMinute ITR, fix the highlighted field, and regenerate JSON.
10. Preview the return.
11. Compare refund/payable and TDS with the app.
12. Submit only when values match.
13. E-verify within 30 days.

## What good looks like

- Portal accepts the JSON without validation errors.
- Refund/payable is close to the app estimate.
- TDS credits match Form 16/26AS.
- You save the acknowledgement number in `/file/done`.

## Stop and ask for CA help if

- Portal rejects capital gains schedules.
- Foreign assets, crypto, F&O, or NRI status appears.
- Refund changes unexpectedly after upload.
